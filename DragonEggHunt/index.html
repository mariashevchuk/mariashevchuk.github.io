Dragon Egg Hunt

Welcome

Five eggs were stolen from the kingdom.
Your job is to go through a series of challenges and retrieve all five stolen eggs.

Instructions

The goal of the each level is to collect an egg and get it to the portal.
There are obstacles that will only let you pass with the egg,
while others will completly block your path.

BEWARE of the evil dragons guarding the eggs!
If you are hit by a dragon, you will lose a life.
Collect gems to gain your lives back.
Remeber not to get greedy, gems cannot be collected if you do not need them!
DON'T swim in the forbidden water!

Your moves are limited, so choose your moves wisely!
